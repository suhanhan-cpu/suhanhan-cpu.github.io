通过尝试

观察m两个相邻的比特位，当他们都是0的时候，有

e(G1,r0∗G1+r1∗G2)=e(G1,G2)r1e(G2,r1∗G1+r2∗G2)=e(G2,G1)r1=e(G1,G2)−r1\begin{array}{l} e(G_1, r_0 * G_1 + r_1 * G_2) = e(G_1, G_2)^{r_1} \\ e(G_2, r_1 * G_1 + r_2 * G_2) = e(G_2, G_1)^{r_1} = e(G_1, G_2)^{-r_1}\\ \end{array} e(G1​,r0​∗G1​+r1​∗G2​)=e(G1​,G2​)r1​e(G2​,r1​∗G1​+r2​∗G2​)=e(G2​,G1​)r1​=e(G1​,G2​)−r1​​
